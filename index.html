This is your analysis — not a description of the problem, but your best explanation of why it is happening. Use the tools below to structure your thinking. Generating summary...
R

Recommendation

What is your plan? What are you going to do about it?

🎯 What you're trying to accomplish

Your recommendation must directly address the root cause you identified in your Assessment. A vague plan is not a plan. Your manager needs to know exactly what will happen, who owns it, and by when — and specifically: when will the problem be resolved? That is the question they will always ask.

✦ Every action needs a named owner (a person or role, not "we") ✦ Every action needs a deadline ✦ Address the root cause directly — not just the symptom ✦ State the specific milestone that confirms the problem is resolved ✦ Include a follow-up check — how will you know the fix is working?
